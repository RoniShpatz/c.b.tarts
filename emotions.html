What's that Emotion?

"What's That Emotion?" is a captivating solo game designed to challenge players' emotional intelligence. With each play, the game generates a random emotion for the player to identify and explore. From the highs of happiness to the lows of frustration, each emotion prompts self-reflection and introspection. Rooted in Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) principles, "What's That Emotion?" encourages players to delve into the underlying thoughts and triggers associated with each feeling, fostering greater self-awareness and emotional regulation.
