About Me

I'm an art therapist with a background in Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and psychotherapy. My passion lies in using art as a medium for healing and self-expression. Through my practice, I aim to facilitate a safe and supportive environment where individuals can explore their emotions, thoughts, and experiences through creative expression.

Combining the principles of CBT with art therapy allows me to help clients identify and challenge negative thought patterns and behaviors while harnessing the transformative power of artistic expression. I believe in the inherent healing potential of art and its ability to access deep-seated emotions and facilitate personal growth.

My approach is client-centered and collaborative, tailoring therapy to meet the unique needs and goals of each individual. Whether it's through painting, drawing, sculpting, or other creative techniques, I work alongside my clients to unlock their inner strengths and foster positive change.

Ultimately, my goal as an art therapist with a CBT perspective is to empower individuals to discover their own innate resources for healing and self-discovery, leading to greater self-awareness, resilience, and well-being.
