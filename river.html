River of Thoughts

Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT) focuses on changing our relationship with thoughts rather than the content of thoughts themselves. In ACT, thoughts are viewed as products of the mind, not necessarily reflective of reality. Instead of trying to control or eliminate unwanted thoughts, ACT teaches individuals to accept them non-judgmentally and allow them to come and go without getting entangled in them. By fostering psychological flexibility, ACT empowers individuals to choose actions aligned with their values, even in the presence of difficult thoughts and emotions. This approach encourages a shift from struggling against thoughts to living a rich and meaningful life despite their presence.
